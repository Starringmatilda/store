Few Care Tips

To keep your piece looking perfect forever

Keep away from direct sunlight

Resin is UV-sensitive, and long exposure to sunlight can cause yellowing over time.

Protect the surface

If using as an ashtray, avoid putting out butts directly on the resin to prevent speed-up of yellowing.

Pro tip: Place a small coin or metallic object in the tapping area to keep it looking new longer.

Clean gently

Use a soft cloth or sponge with mild handwashing soap or window cleaner. Avoid abrasive cleaners or rough scrubbing pads.

Hope you enjoy your piece!

FAQS

What is epoxy resin?
Resin is a material that starts as a liquid and hardens into a very durable, shiny, and smooth solid and becomes similar to a strong plastic or glass. It allows us to preserve specific colors, glitters, and images inside your ashtray/piece forever.
Is it toxic?
No. Once the resin has fully hardened (cured), it becomes chemically inert. This means it is non-toxic and completely safe to have in your home.
Is it safe to use?
Yes, it is safe to handle and use daily! (Note: While it is safe to touch, standard resin is generally not recommended for eating food directly off of.)
Can it break?
Cured resin is much stronger and more durable than glass or ceramic, making it shatter-resistant. It won't break if you accidentally knock it over on a table. However, it is not indestructible; if dropped from a high place onto a hard floor or handled very roughly, it could chip or crack.
Will my ashtray melt or burn?
These ashtrays are heat-resistant, meaning they can handle hot ash safely. However, they are not fireproof. To keep them looking new, please do not leave a burning smokables resting on the resin for a long time or stub it out too forcefully, as extreme heat can eventually leave a mark.
How do I care for it?
Cleaning is easy! Wash gently with cool water and mild soap (such as dish soap), then dry with a soft cloth. A full care guide will be included with your package.
Can I customize everything?
Absolutely! You have full control over the design. You pick the shape, the colors, whether you want glitter (and what color), and you can send me any image you want embedded inside.
How long will my order take?
Ideally, the process takes 3 days to pour, dry, and polish your item. If there are any delays, I will let you know immediately.
